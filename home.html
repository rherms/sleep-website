Who we are

We are a group of Stanford students developing a vineyard monitoring system using drones. Our goal is to create an all-in-one solution that can detect common problems in vineyards and automatically report them to you.

  • Learn more

What we do

We are building an automated system to survey a vineyard, identify problems with crops, and notify you of issues that need attention. We are turning this system into an all-in-one package that can help you with all your vineyard problems.

On-demand vineyard monitoring

Send your drone out to the other side of your vineyard to see what's happening. You'll get real-time footage of your vineyard.

Vigor detection

Our state of the art computer vision detects the level of vigor in each part of your vineyard and gives you a map.

Mite detection

We tell you when your vineyard is affected by mites so that you can much more quickly save the area.

Pest control

Constantly finding wild animals on your vineyard? No worried. Crevyce will detect and scare them away for you.

Temperature and weather reports

We provide you with data on temperature and weather trends and how they affect your crops.

Vineyard analytics software

Our easy to use, web-based analytics software will keep you informed and provide you with insight.
